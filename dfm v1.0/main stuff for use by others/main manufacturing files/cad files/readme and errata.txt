There is a flat on the bladeside coupler in the printable version that should be removed. This is corrected in the moldable one.

Some orings are usually used on the three armed coupler between the flywheel and the bladeside piece. These may be optional/useless I don't know yet.

The wavy washer is not shown in the cad file, I just didn't get it in here yet.  There should be a wavy/Belleville washer between the shaft clamp and the spacer that interfaces to the inner race of the bearing.  IT may make sense to have a normal washer on either side of the wavy washer.  Without this, the TPU creeps and a small knocking sound can arise intermittently.
