# Big-Quiet-Fan
This is an axial fan with a blade that is 500 mm diameter (frame is a 550 mm square basically), that has unprecedented flow to noise ratio at pressures useful in air purification appliances, and presumably many other things.  But it's primary application is for making Extra Quiet Corsi-Rosenthal boxes (EQ-CR boxes).  Basically, they get twice the CADR with far less noise than a lasko 3733 fan on low (about 950 CFM CADR with a typical filter set, at 41 dBa noise (400 rpm), so about 9-10 dB less noise).  It's also extremely quite at lower speeds while still getting very good flow, so it's good for a bedroom to prevent allergies, for example.
